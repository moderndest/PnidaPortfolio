INTRODUCTION

Muppo The Little Witch is a story about an adventure of mushroom witch and her friend "Katie"( a half cat and caterpillar) in the big forest.

Muppo and Katie

Muppo A clumsy and funny witch who love to explore. She has a mushroom spirits (ทำไรได้). everywhere she goes, she always make a troble to every spirits of forest.
Katie a caterpillar who have a dream to be a butterfly. but somthing stop him to make his dream come true easily.

Mushroom Spirit

A little spirit of forest who live and protect the forest. they look like children and love to play around.

The art style is inspired from an illustration for children book.
