INTRODUCTION

Muppo The Little Witch is a story about an adventure of mushroom witch and her friend "Katie"( a half cat and caterpillar) in the big forest.

Muppo and Katie

Muppo A clumsy and funny witch who love to explore the world. She has a mushroom spirits to help the forest. Everywhere she goes, she always make a troble to every spirits of forest.

Katie A caterpillar who have a dream to be a butterfly. But somthing stop him to make his dream become true easily.

Mushroom Spirit

A little spirit of forest who is a gardian of the forest. They look like children and love to play around.

The art style is inspired from an illustration from children book.

Bettie

A fierce bat but kind sometime. Bettie is inspired from bat and vampire.

Daisy Depp

A shy turtle who always hide his body with his daisy hat and his shell. Daisy Depp is inspired from turtle and kappa
