THE WHALE’S COMIMG

The concept of The Whale’s Coming is when this creature comes to the crypto ocean. This gargantuan animal always attracts every nearby small creature. I have been inspired by individuals and institutions who hold large amounts of coins of a certain crypto currency known as whales in the crypto world. I designed the Etheruem coin like crystalized air because, similarly to the air that every life form breathes, this substance has high value. For the Ocean, I was inspired by the sci-fi world. So, I utilized neon lights and holograms. The sound is an electronic pathway to a deep sea experience.
