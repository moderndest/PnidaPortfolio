TO THE MOON

The concept is that Elon Musk and his dogs are preparing themselves to go to the moon in their supercool rocket. I got this idea from Elon Musk often saying that he will take Dogecoin to the moon. I wanted my work to stand out using Shiba memes and cute animations. I also included Shiba sounds and music because I wanted people who saw this art to have an enjoyable experience from my work.
