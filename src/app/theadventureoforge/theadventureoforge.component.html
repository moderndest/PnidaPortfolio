THE ADVENTURE OF ORGE
(PERSONAL PROJECT)

A collection of work I have done for The Adventures of ogre, a new world building Project I am working on! I will update this collection in the future, but just thought I would share this for people who were interested.

The ogre boy and the Mom creature

“The Child and The Creature” is the story of one boy named “Tone”, who has half the blood of a human and an ogre. He cannot live in the human world since humans do not accept his identity. He has only one trusted pet named “Mom”. “Mom” is a legendary creature from Himmaparn forest and it is the reason why he knows his special ability, which is an ability to communicate to all species. with this ability, it makes Tone differ from normal humans and humans consider this ability as dark magic. One day, the human world has a strange event that causes food shortage and catastrophe. He believes that this event can be stopped by accessing Tribhum world (Tribhum world is the world that combines 3 mystery levels in Buddhism including devil layer, angel layer, and spirit layer). The way to get into Tribhum is very dangerous and whoever enters this world never comes back. Since he wants to be accepted by humans, he decides to go into the Tribhum world. That is the beginning of his journey. He has to use his ability to solve the problem and apply it in the human world.

mom

is a legendary creature from Himmaparn forest. Mom's body shape is similar to a combination of dog, cat, gecko, chameleon, and monkey. Mom has a long body and reptile scales. Mom has a very good ear to hear many 1000 miles and also can climb like a monkey.

other Character

TOSSHIRIWON

is the commander of ogre in Tribhum world. He is very strict, strong and powerful. He has 4 arms and his weapon is a club. He has more than 1000 ogres under his control. One day, He see Tone sneak into Tribhum world and he considers Tone as a threat. So, he tried to catch Tone from then on.

ENVIRONMENTAL DESIGN

This is environment design in the adventure of ogre. I design in Thai fantasy world. I have inspired from Thai culture.

TRIPHUM

Triphum is a 3 layers world in buddhism including demon world, angel world, spirit world. This world can be accessible by humans. This is a world that Tone will take a journey to save the world.

This is creature designs in the Triphum’s world. This creature’s signature is a Kanok pattern which is a Thai art pattern on their body such as fin, horn, tentacle, wing, scales or fur. The reason why they have a Kanok pattern is because the place that they live in is full of crystal’s mineral which are powerful.
